# Presidential Powers: Then vs. Now

This visual guide compares the powers of the current U.S. presidency with the presidency in this draft, showing what remains strong, what is narrowed, and what new accountability limits apply.

## How To Read This Guide

Each row compares one presidential power or constraint. Use the category buttons to focus on core executive powers, appointments and administration, emergency and war powers, or accountability limits.

## What This Guide Covers

- veto power and executive orders
- appointments, acting officials, and removals
- emergency declarations and Commander in Chief limits
- pardons and clemency restrictions
- recall, impeachment, and the end of categorical immunity

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.
