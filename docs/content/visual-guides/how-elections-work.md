# How Elections Work

This visual guide explains what is different about federal elections under this constitution — the voting method, how registration works, what protections prevent manipulation, and how voters can recall officials.

## How To Read This Guide

Each card covers one feature of the electoral system. Use the category buttons to focus on voting methods, access and registration, integrity protections, or the recall mechanism.

## What This Guide Covers

- ranked-choice voting and multi-member districts for the House
- instant runoff voting for the Regional Assembly
- automatic voter registration
- election date protections against emergency manipulation
- independent redistricting with anti-gerrymandering rules
- voter recall of House and Regional Assembly members

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.

For exact wording and legal detail, read:

- [Article I](articles/I-electoral-system.md)
- [Article XII](articles/XII-constitutional-organs.md)
