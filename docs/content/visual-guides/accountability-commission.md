# The Accountability Commission

This visual guide explains what the Accountability Commission is, what it can and cannot do, and what protections prevent it from being captured, defunded, or shut down.

## How To Read This Guide

Each card covers one aspect of the Commission. Use the category buttons to focus on composition, powers, limits, or the structural protections that keep it independent.

## What This Guide Covers

- how the Commission is composed and appointed
- independence protections that no president or Congress can override
- the full list of what it can investigate, prosecute, and enforce
- what it is explicitly prohibited from doing
- what happens automatically if someone tries to capture, defund, or paralyze it

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.

For exact wording and legal detail, read:

- [Article XII](articles/XII-constitutional-organs.md)
- [Article III](articles/III-executive.md)
