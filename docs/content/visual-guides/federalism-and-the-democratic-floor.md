# Federalism And The Democratic Floor

This visual guide explains the constitutional line between preserved state autonomy and the national democratic floor that states cannot lawfully violate.

## How To Read This Guide

The guide has four sections:

- what states still control
- what states may not do
- federal and judicial backstops
- local democracy and preemption

Use the category buttons to focus on one part of the federalism structure at a time.

## What This Guide Covers

- preserved state authority
- equal citizenship and rights floors
- anti-backsliding limits on state autonomy
- federal judicial relief against unconstitutional state action
- local democracy and state preemption boundaries

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.

For exact wording and legal detail, read:

- [Article X](articles/X-federalism.md)
- [Article V](articles/V-rights.md)
- [Article IX](articles/IX-citizenship-membership.md)
