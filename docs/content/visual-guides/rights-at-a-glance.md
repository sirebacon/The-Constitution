# Rights At A Glance

This visual guide groups the constitution's major rights into a few readable categories so first-time readers can understand the system without starting from article-by-article legal text.

## How To Read This Guide

Each card represents a major rights cluster. The short description explains the plain-language function of the right, and the article reference shows where the binding text lives.

Use the category buttons to focus on one slice of the rights structure at a time, or view all categories together.

## What This Guide Covers

- political rights
- civil liberties
- due process
- equality
- digital rights
- social and economic rights
- non-derogable rights

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.
