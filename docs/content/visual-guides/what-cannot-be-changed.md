# What Cannot Be Changed

This visual guide isolates the Constitution's unamendable core: the commitments that cannot be repealed, suspended, or emptied out by any amendment path.

## How To Read This Guide

The guide has four sections:

- democratic structure
- equal citizenship and rights floor
- guardrail institutions
- constitutional order and civilian rule

Use the category buttons to focus on one part of the locked democratic core at a time.

## What This Guide Covers

- the permanently protected democratic minimum
- why equal citizenship is locked
- why judicial review and Constitutional Organs are protected
- why civilian control and peaceful transfer are beyond amendment
- why the amendment power cannot legally unlock itself

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.

For exact wording and legal detail, read:

- [Article XI](articles/XI-amendments.md)
- [Clause Note: The Unamendable Core](commentary/clauses/unamendable-core.md)
