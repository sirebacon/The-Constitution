# The Amendment Process

This visual guide explains how this constitution can be changed, how the two amendment tracks differ, and what cannot be changed under any process.

## How To Read This Guide

The comparison table shows Track 1 and Track 2 side by side. The unamendable core section lists every provision that cannot be amended, repealed, or suspended by any majority at any time.

## What This Guide Covers

- Track 1 (structural amendments): Congress vote, pre-clearance, state ratification
- Track 2 (rights expansion): Congress vote, state ratification, national referendum
- The unamendable core: eleven provisions that are permanently locked
- What happens if pre-clearance is denied
- Comparison to the current U.S. Article V amendment process

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.

For exact wording and legal detail, read:

- [Article XI](articles/XI-amendments.md)
