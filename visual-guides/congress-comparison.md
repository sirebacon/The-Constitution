# Congress: Then vs. Now

This visual guide shows how the legislature in this draft compares to the current U.S. Congress — what changed, what stayed the same, and why.

## How To Read This Guide

Each row compares one structural feature. Use the category buttons to focus on composition, powers, accountability, or process differences.

## What This Guide Covers

- chamber composition and representation
- term lengths and election cycles
- exclusive powers of each chamber
- accountability and removal rules
- procedural rules including the filibuster
- confirmation and treaty powers

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.
