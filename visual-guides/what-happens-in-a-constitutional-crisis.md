# What Happens In A Constitutional Crisis

This visual guide shows how the constitutional system is supposed to react when a crisis stops being ordinary politics and turns into a fight over lawful authority itself.

## How To Read This Guide

The guide has three paths:

- election and certification crisis
- emergency abuse crisis
- institutional defiance crisis

Use the path buttons to focus on one crisis chain at a time.

## What This Guide Covers

- election certification deadlines
- transition and continuity duties
- emergency approval and automatic lapse rules
- judicial review and anti-delay backstops
- anti-subversion consequences for obstruction and defiance

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.
