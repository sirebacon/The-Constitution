# From Election To Transfer Of Power

This visual guide shows how the constitutional system moves from voting to certification to a lawful transfer of power, and what protections prevent delay, sabotage, or refusal to hand over authority.

## How To Read This Guide

The guide has three phases:

- vote and count
- certification and disputes
- transition and transfer

Use the phase buttons to focus on one part of the chain at a time.

## What This Guide Covers

- multi-day voting and early voting
- Electoral Commission administration and certification
- judicial limits on delay tactics
- transition duties of the outgoing government
- records, support, and continuity obligations
- anti-subversion consequences for obstructing transfer

## What This Guide Does Not Do

This guide is an orientation layer, not the constitutional text itself.
