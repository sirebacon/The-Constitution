# Clause Note: War Powers Backstops

## Why Not Rely On Ordinary Political Pressure?

Because unauthorized military action often survives through drift, ambiguity, and institutional hesitation.

If the constitutional system does nothing more than register disapproval, the executive can often continue the operation while the rest of government argues about it.

## Why The Draft Uses Concrete Consequences

This draft links failed authorization to downstream consequences like:

- withdrawal duty
- funding consequences
- impeachment-triggering pathways

The goal is to stop unauthorized war from becoming self-justifying merely because troops are already deployed.

## Why This Matters For Democracy

War is one of the clearest areas where timing can defeat accountability.

The backstop architecture exists to make democratic authorization real instead of ceremonial.
