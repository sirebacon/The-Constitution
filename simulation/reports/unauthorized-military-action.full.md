# Unauthorized Sustained Military Action
Scenario: unauthorized-military-action.json

## Timeline
- Day 0: EVENT - President initiates military action without prior authorization. [Article XVI Section 1]
- Day 0: OBLIGATION - Congress must grant authorization for continued military force or refuse it by day 30. [Article XVI Section 1]
- Day 0: SCENARIO - The President initiates sustained military operations without authorization, Congress does not pass an AUMF within 30 days, and that failure counts as nonauthorization for all purposes of Article XVI. The President then ignores the required withdrawal order. The scenario tests the war-powers backstop under pressure, including the self-executing fiscal hold chain under Article XVI Section 1.5(d).
- Day 31: BOTTLENECK - Congress failed to grant authorization for continued military force or refuse it by day 30 under Article XVI Section 1. [Article XVI Section 1]
- Day 31: EVENT - Congress does not provide timely authorization for the military action. [Article XVI Section 1]
- Day 31: OBLIGATION - Chief Justice must issue the required withdrawal order for unauthorized continued operations by day 36. [Article XVI Section 1.5(c)]
- Day 34: RESOLUTION - Chief Justice issued a withdrawal order. [Article XVI Section 1.5(c)]
- Day 40: OBLIGATION - Accountability Commission must issue a self-executing fiscal hold on appropriated funds for the unlawful operation by day 41. [Article XVI Section 1.5(d)]
- Day 40: OBLIGATION - House of Representatives must hold a recorded impeachment vote on the unlawful continued military operation by day 61. [Article XVI Section 1.5(e) and Article III Section 10.2A]
- Day 40: VIOLATION - President continues military operations in violation of a withdrawal order. [Article XVI Section 8(e)]
- Day 41: OBLIGATION - Secretary of the Treasury must implement the ACC fiscal hold within 24 hours by day 42. [Article XVI Section 1.5(d)]
- Day 41: RESOLUTION - Accountability Commission issued a self-executing fiscal hold on appropriated funds for the unlawful operation. [Article XVI Section 1.5(d)]
- Day 42: RESOLUTION - Secretary of the Treasury implemented the ACC fiscal hold, cutting off appropriated funds for the unlawful operation. [Article XVI Section 1.5(d)]
- Day 65: BOTTLENECK - House of Representatives failed to hold a recorded impeachment vote on the unlawful continued military operation by day 61 under Article XVI Section 1.5(e) and Article III Section 10.2A. [Article XVI Section 1.5(e) and Article III Section 10.2A]
- Day 65: OBLIGATION - Regional Assembly must proceed to impeachment trial on the automatically transmitted war-powers articles by day 86. [Article III Section 10.2A and Section 10.3]
- Day 65: OUTCOME - The certified war-powers charge is automatically transmitted to the Regional Assembly as articles of impeachment limited to the certified violation. [Article III Section 10.2A]
- Day 65: RESOLUTION - Regional Assembly began trial on automatically transmitted war-powers articles. [Article III Section 10.2A and Section 10.3]
