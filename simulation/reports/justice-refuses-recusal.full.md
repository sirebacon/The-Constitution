# Justice Refuses Valid Recusal Demand
Scenario: justice-refuses-recusal.json

## Timeline
- Day 0: SCENARIO - A party petitions the Judicial Conduct Board to require a justice's recusal based on a financial conflict of interest. The Board directs recusal; the justice refuses. The refusal triggers an automatic referral and the justice is barred from the case under Art IV §7.3.
- Day 1: EVENT - A party petitions the Judicial Conduct Board for recusal of Associate Justice on the ground of undisclosed financial interest in a regulated entity before the Court, pursuant to Article IV Section 7.3. [Article IV Section 7.3]
- Day 1: OBLIGATION - Judicial Conduct Board must rule on the recusal petition and direct the justice to recuse if the petition meets the standard by day 15. [Article IV Section 7.3]
- Day 10: OBLIGATION - Judicial Conduct Board must process the automatic recusal-refusal referral and determine whether removal proceedings are warranted by day 40. [Article IV Section 7.3]
- Day 10: RESOLUTION - Judicial Conduct Board directed recusal; the justice refused, triggering automatic referral and case bar under Article IV Section 7.3. [Article IV Section 7.3]
- Day 10: VIOLATION - Associate Justice refuses to recuse despite a valid Judicial Conduct Board direction under Article IV Section 7.3. The refusal triggers an automatic referral and the justice is barred from the case. [Article IV Section 7.3]
- Day 35: OUTCOME - Refusal to comply with a valid recusal direction is independently reviewable conduct under Article IV Section 7.3. The justice is barred from the case regardless of the removal outcome. [Article IV Section 7.3 and Section 8.1]
- Day 35: RESOLUTION - Judicial Conduct Board processed the recusal-refusal referral and referred the matter for impeachment proceedings as conduct-based grounds for removal. [Article IV Section 7.3]
