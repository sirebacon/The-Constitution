# Member of Congress Takes Bribes and Chamber Leadership Shields Them

## Timeline
- Day 0: OBLIGATION - Accountability Commission must investigate and prosecute the congressional bribery matter notwithstanding any chamber preference for internal handling. [Article II Section 14.2 and Article VIII Section 5.1]
- Day 0: SCENARIO - A senior member of Congress accepts money and future consulting promises from a regulated industry in exchange for blocking oversight hearings and weakening pending legislation. Chamber leadership refuses to move on expulsion and argues that discipline is exclusively internal. The Accountability Commission opens a corruption case anyway, and the member is later convicted of bribery. The scenario tests whether chamber shielding can block outside constitutional enforcement.
