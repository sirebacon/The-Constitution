# Government Withdraws Shelter Protections Without Replacement
Scenario: housing-floor-failure.json

## Timeline

- Day 1: OBLIGATION - Federal courts must preserve existing shelter protections against backsliding and require the government to adopt a constitutionally sufficient replacement or maintain existing protections pending adoption of an adequate alternative by day 31. - Day 25: RESOLUTION - Federal courts preserved existing shelter protections against backsliding and required the government to adopt constitutionally sufficient replacement measures within 120 days; interim executive use of existing lawful authority ordered. [Article XVIII Section 2.3 and Section 2.4]
