# Nuclear First-Use Order — Secretary Refusal and Circumvention Attempt
Scenario: nuclear-first-use-circumvention.json

## Timeline
- Day 0: EVENT - President orders first use of nuclear weapons without a prior nuclear attack on the United States. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: OBLIGATION - Secretary of Defense must confirm or refuse the nuclear first-use order by day 1. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: OBLIGATION - Secretary of State must confirm or refuse the nuclear first-use order by day 1. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: OUTCOME - Nuclear first-use order cannot proceed. Under Article XI Section 3.1, the order does not proceed if either Secretary declines to confirm it. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: RESOLUTION - Secretary of Defense refused to confirm the nuclear first-use order, blocking the launch under Article XI Section 3.1. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: RESOLUTION - Secretary of State refused to confirm the nuclear first-use order. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 0: SCENARIO - The President orders first use of nuclear weapons without a prior nuclear attack on the United States. The Secretary of Defense and Secretary of State both refuse to confirm the order, blocking the launch under Article XI Section 3.1. The President fires the Secretary of Defense to install a compliant replacement. The acting Secretary also refuses. The scenario tests the multi-party authorization requirement and confirms that removal of a refusing Secretary does not clear the constitutional block.
- Day 1: OBLIGATION - Acting Secretary of Defense must confirm or refuse the nuclear first-use order by day 2. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 1: VIOLATION - President removes the Secretary of Defense to install a replacement willing to confirm a nuclear first-use order, using the removal power to circumvent a constitutional authorization safeguard. [Article XI Section 3.1 and Article III Section 4.4]
- Day 2: OUTCOME - The nuclear first-use order remains blocked. The President's removal of the Secretary of Defense did not produce a compliant replacement. No authorized officer has confirmed the order. [Article XI Section 3.1]
- Day 2: RESOLUTION - Acting Secretary of Defense also refused to confirm the nuclear first-use order; the launch remains blocked. [Article XI Section 3.1]
