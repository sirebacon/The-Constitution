# Code of Conduct

This repository is a public constitutional drafting project for peaceful civic use.

Participants are expected to keep discussion and contributions:

- respectful
- nonviolent
- legally cautious
- open to criticism and revision
- focused on constitutional design, commentary, translation, simulation, documentation, or site work

## Unacceptable Conduct

The following are not allowed in this project:

- threats or intimidation
- advocacy of violence
- sabotage or operational planning
- encouragement of imminent unlawful action
- harassment or targeted abuse
- attempts to use the repository as a vehicle for coercion or force

Content or behavior in those categories may be removed.

## Project Scope

This project exists for constitutional drafting, critique, education, translation, simulation, and democratic advocacy.

It is not a militia, an operational organization, or a platform for unlawful action.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, or limit participation when needed to keep the project within its peaceful civic scope.
